The Legend of Kelee

A Classic Love for Baguettes

Kelee was born from a simple desire: to share the sensory magic of a perfect Vietnamese baguette. Our recipe combines classic French baking methods introduced to Vietnam centuries ago with the vibrant, bold, and fresh ingredients of Saigon streets.

The Perfect Bake

High moisture dough and high-heat steam baking give our baguettes their wafer-thin, shatteringly crisp outer crust and cloud-soft interior.

Homemade Pâté & Mayo

We spread our rich chicken liver pâté and creamy egg mayo on every baguette—made fresh in our kitchen daily.
